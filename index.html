About Juan
AKA Juandicimo the Great

Story

I am the great Juandicimo, magician extraordinaire, but it wasn't always this way. Once upon a time I loved simply to make food, play Call of Duty, and hang out with my beautiful, loving family. But one day, I was bitten by a magical tuna fish, and blessed with extraordinary magical abilities. Since then I've been basically doing the same things, only magically. Some people say now that I cheat at video games and that no food could possibly taste this good, but let me tell you...anything is possible!
